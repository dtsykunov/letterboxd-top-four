Pick Your Favorites

Can't decide which of the films you've watched on Letterboxd are your true favorites? This tool runs a series of head-to-head matchups and ranks your top 4.

  1. Export your data from letterboxd.com/settings/data.
  2. Upload the downloaded .zip, or just the watched.csv inside it.
  3. Pick the film you prefer in each head-to-head matchup.
  4. Get your ranked top 4.

How it works

You'll see two films at a time. Just pick the one you like more. A single loss doesn't knock a film out.

How are the four actually chosen?
  1. Find #1 with a knockout. Films are shown two at a time, and you keep the one you like more. Winners move on and play again, round after round, until one film has beaten everything in its path. That's your favorite.
  2. Give the runners-up a second chance. Every film whose single loss was to your favorite comes back and plays off. Whoever survives takes second place.
  3. Repeat for #3 and #4. Each remaining place is filled the same way.

Everything happens in your browser. Your data never leaves this page.
